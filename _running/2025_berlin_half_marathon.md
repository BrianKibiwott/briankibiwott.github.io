The Berlin Marathon 2025 was a memorable race and a personal milestone, as I achieved a new marathon personal best.

Berlin is renowned for its fast course, enthusiastic crowds, and rich marathon history, making it a special venue for chasing a personal record. The race was the culmination of months of training, balancing research, work, and running.

Beyond the result itself, the experience reinforced many of the lessons that make running such an important part of my life: consistency, patience, resilience, and the value of long-term goals.

**Result:** Personal Best (PB)
